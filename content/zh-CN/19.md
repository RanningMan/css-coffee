codeSnippet: ".box {
  width: 100px;
  height: 100px;
  background-color: blue;
  animation: example 2s infinite;
  animation-timing-function: ease-in-out;
}
@keyframes example {
  0% {transform: scale(1);}
  50% {transform: scale(1.2);}
  100% {transform: scale(1);}
}"
---

CSS中的'animation-timing-function'属性定义了动画的中间值是如何计算的。它通过指定速度曲线来决定动画的节奏。
